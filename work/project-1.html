HEURISTIC EVALUATION REPORT 启发式评估报告

This report provides a detailed analysis of the heuristic evaluation process used to evaluate WBY mobile application. The evaluation itself was performed using the heuristic evaluation usability method, based on heuristics provided by Jakob Nielsen. This method consists of evaluators comparing a pre-defined set of usability principles to an application while attempting to complete a system task.

报告用于评估微播易APP提供了一个启发性评估过程的细节分析。评估本身是使用启发式评估可用性方法，基于Jakob Nielsen提供的启发式方法。 这个方法由评估人员将一组预定义的可用性原则与应用程序，同时尝试完成系统任务。

Problem Summery of Heuristic Evaluation 启发式评估的问题总结

1. The first use process is unclear because there was not an indication for the user when they finished the sign-up experience. They do not know what the next step is.
2. Low retention when the user finished an order because they could do nothing but wait.
3. Too many statuses of order, some of them are useless. The media specialist would help the user to finish the operation on the application or the company system.
4. No one wants to fill out the account information because they do not know the importance of filling out.
5. User cannot find the expiration account on the account management collection because there are too many accounts. It is hard to find.

1. 用户无法及时得知该页面的状态与作用。
2. 页面信息无主次，用户无法识别重点。
3. 与用户心理预期不符的操作。
4. 复杂的订单状态及混乱的操作行为按钮。
5. 用户无法感知的专有名词定义。
6. 空白状态造成用户困惑。

USABILITY TESTING 可用性测试

Usability test is a test performed on users to get their feedback in terms of their behavior on a product. There are three main types: in-person, remote and unmonitored. Each method has its own advantages and disadvantages.Standing at the point for designing the next version of the app, a usability test was proposed to understand general user experience and find the possible improvements.

可用性测试是对用户执行的一项测试，目的是获得用户对产品行为的反馈。 主要有三种类型：面对面，远程和不受监视。 每种方法都有其自身的优点和缺点。站在设计应用程序下一版本的关键时刻，提出了可用性测试以了解一般用户体验并找到可能的改进。

I. User Interview 用户访谈

We interviewed stakeholders from our project team. They use WBY application as a daily tool, and some of them are first time user. We conducted two rounds of usability testing focused on finding usability issues, observing signup experiences, getting feedback, and making improvement and enhancements on existing and new features. We aggregated and analyzed our test results. I was able to redesign user workflow and made iterations based on analytic reports from user interview and usability testings.

我们采访了我们项目组的利益相关者(如：媒体专员、达人等)。他们使用WBY应用程序作为日常工具，其中一些是第一次用户。我们进行了两轮可用性测试，重点是发现可用性问题、观察注册体验、获得反馈、对现有和新特性进行改进和增强。我们汇总并分析了我们的测试结果。我能够重新设计用户工作流，并根据用户访谈和可用性测试的分析报告进行迭代。

KEY FINDINGS 关键发现

Based on our interview, we analyzed interview results

For Influencer 达人:

根据我们的访谈，我们分析访得出3个主要观点：
1、寻找更多的商业机会；
2，快速提现；
3、渴望简单易用的软件

Behaviors Document 行为文档

major changes in the new design

I. Signup/Login process - step by step signup/login vs. combined form 注册/登陆流程-逐步注册/登陆vs.合并表单

I designed a step by step process for the user. This allows the user to focus more attention on the current page, and also users can add platform when they first login the application. According to the research, we found that the user could not know what to do for the next step. They thought they were fools.

1. 根据现实业务需求，重设计分注册、登录流程 - 用户通过网页注册，工作人员核查通过后，用户将受到通过邮件，随后用户可登陆APP；
2.我为用户设计了一个循序渐进的过程。这使得用户可以将更多的注意力放在当前页面上，并且用户可以在首次登陆后添加平台。根据研究，我们发现用户不知道下一步该做什么。他们认为自己很傻；
3.视觉重设计


II. Adding Account 添加账号功能

1. "Add account" process optimization to improve user experience.
2. "Complete Information" page information optimization.
3. After the user first login process, add the "Add Account" process to guide.

1.「添加账号」流程优化，提高用户体验
2.「完善信息」页面信息优化
3.用户初次登录流程后，添加「添加账号」流程引导


III. “Order” related pages “订单”相关页面

Standardization process for users, improve user efficiency; reduce internal consumption for enterprises.
1. "Order Status" is simplified to help users quickly browse the content and find everything they need without any hits.
2. "Order details" optimization, users can easily complete tasks during the initial use, improve user satisfaction.
3. "MY" refactoring, adding revenue board, my order, emergency agency section

标准化流程对达人用户，提高用户效率；对企业，减少内部消耗。
1.「订单状态」简化，帮助用户快速浏览内容并找到所需的一切，而不会受到任何打击
2.「订单详情」优化，用户在初次使用过程中可以轻松完成任务，提高用户满意度
3.「我」重构，新增收益板、我的订单、紧急代办板块


IV. Account Management page 账号管理

Complete account information is an essential component for the user to increase their business opportunities.
1. Newly added "switch" function, users can quickly distinguish "downline account", timely content modification, reduce business losses.
2. Account display - Card type instead of list design, users can quickly and accurately find the target account.
3. Account information packaging - Recommend more and more accurate business opportunities for users. This part modifies the account information classification, interactive redesign, increases user readability, and is easy for users to operate.
4. Add user guidance - Through marketing means, urge users to complete account information.

完整的账户信息是用户增加商业机会的重要组成部分。
1.新添“开关”功能，用户可快速区分“下架账号”，及时进行内容修改，减小商业损失；
2.账号展示 - 卡片式代替列表式设计，用户可快速精准找到目标账号；
3.账号信息包装 - 为用户推荐更多更准确的商机。此部分修改账号信息分类、交互重设计，增加用户易读性、易于用户操作；
4.新增用户引导 - 通过市场营销手段，督促用户完账号善信息


V. Mission Hall 任务大厅

Through the analysis of the interview results of the usability test, users hope to find more business opportunities. Through the “task hall”, the reach users will be passive and active, and they are free to find more business opportunities.
1. "Recommended" - the platform information added by the ultimate person and the "information perfect" content category, determine the recommended content.
2. Users can customize the navigation content classification.
3. The requested task will appear in "My Order".

通过可用性测试的访谈结果分析，用户希望寻找更多的商机，通过“任务大厅”，达人用户将化被动为主动，可自由寻找更多的商业机会。
1."推荐" - 根绝达人添加的平台信息和“信息完善”内容内类，决定推荐内容；
2.用户可自定义导航内容分类；
3.申请的任务将会在“我的订单”中出现
