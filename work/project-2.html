enterprise internal office system - Influencer account information optimization

企业内部办公系统 - 达人账号信息优化
Project Overview 项目概览

Babysitter(BS) is an enterprise internal office system, and it also contains some functions of Customer Relationship Management( CRM) product. The employee can use the system to deal with their business stuff, for example, expand the resource of influencers, select influencers, check orders, adjust the price, combine the business orders, etc.

系统B是微播易自主研发的网络系统，用于企业内部各部门员工处理工作事物的系统，其中包含了部分CRM功能，用于管理客户关系。大部分功能用于处理交易订单全流程。其中包含了，拓号、选号、质检、调价、订单合成等功能。

Problem 问题

The advertiser's selection number will have special requirements. Currently, the content information is not supported in the account information. Most of the information is provided by the media manager in the execl. This has led to an increase in the workload of media managers, a long project cycle and high resource consumption.

广告主选号会有特殊要求，目前账号信息内不支持这些内容整理，大部分信息提供主要由媒介经理整理在execl中。导致媒介经理工作量增加，项目周期长，资源消耗大。

Purpose 目的

- To improve the accuracy of the advertiser's platform selection
- Improve the order rate of the people
- Reduce the workload of the staff

为提高广告主平台选号准确性、提高达人接单率、减小工作人员工作量

Solution 解决方法

- Information configuration
- Use marketing methods to encourage people to improve information

信息配置化、使用市场营销手段鼓励达人完善信息

My Contributions 我的贡献

My role in this project is a User Experience Designer and Visual Designer.

我的职责是用户体验设计和视觉设计。

I used two weeks to conduct research, flow chart, interview, user testing, wireframes and final visual design. I was able to revised the design based on user feedbacks before the application was launched.

我花了2周时间进行调研、流程图和用户测试、线框图和最终的视觉设计。在应用程序启动之前，我能够根据用户反馈修改设计。
